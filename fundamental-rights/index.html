Fundamental Rights of American Democracy

Dignity of the Individual

What does it mean?

Dignity of the Individual means that everyone has inherent and inalienable value.

Where does it come from?

It comes from the philosophical, humanistic, or religious belief that everyone has equal value regardless of social status.
